CAPS-PSRL Summer Internship 2020


The Centre for Advanced Parallel Systems(CAPS) - Parallel Systems Research Lab(PSRL), PES University, announces the Summer Internship Program seeking exceptionally bright, motivated and committed students for exciting and challenging summer 2020 opportunities for R & D Projects in parallel systems including (but not limited to) the following areas:

  • Parallelising and Accelerating on CPU/GPUs/Accelerators

    • Modern Audio, Video Image Codecs
    • Machine Learning and Deep Learning Algorithms
    • Large Scale Social Network Graphs and Data Analytics
    • Trees, Graphs, Strings algorithms
    • Cryptography and Blockchain Algorithms

  • Parallel Analytics

    • Privacy Preserving Data Analytics
    • Data/Media Analytics for Smart Classrooms, Smart City, Smart Planet

  • Automatic Detection and Correction

    • Concurrency Bugs (Data Race, Deadlocks)
    • Memory Bugs (leaks, Bloats)
    • Functionality/Performance Bugs
    • Plagiarism

  • Parallel Programming Frameworks and Tools

    • Pthreads
    • OpenMP
    • CUDA
    • OpenCL
    • Intel TBB
    • MPI

  • Ad-hoc mobile many core compute clusters

    • Discovering
    • Managing
    • Scheduling/Load-Balancing
    • Fault Tolerance

Please Note

  • The summer internship is open to bright and motivated B. E./ B. Tech. students entering second year, third year and fourth year this summer 2020
  • Each intern will be assigned a senior student mentor as well as faculty member for guidence during the internship for continuous progress.
  • Interns will be expected to spend at the least 2 months at CAPS-PSRL; the exact dates may be decided in consultation with their respective internship mentors and guide.
  • The interns will have to make their own boarding and lodging arrangements.
  • It is preferable that the interns use their own laptops for their project; networking and internet resources will be provided by the lab.
  • The last date for applying is March 31, 2020.
  • All information including GPA entered in your application is subject to verification
  • Please provide email of at least one reference for seeking recommendation letter directly
  • The decision of the selection and execution of internships by CAPS-PSRL will be final
